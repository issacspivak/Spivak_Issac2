Basic Passing Tutorial

1. Aim your left analog stick to the desired target

Point the left analog stick to the player that you want to pass it to

2. Press the "A" Button

Press the green button with the letter "A" on your xbox controller

                                  

By either tapping or holding down the A button, you can adjust the power and distance of the pass

3. Through Balls

If you want to lead a pass in front of your player instead of passing it directly to him, use a through ball by pressing the "Y" button on the controller.

                                  

If your player is making a run behind the defence, use the chip through ball to float the leading pass over the defenders by pressing the "LB" button on the conroller while you press Y.
